*While our curation process at ArtHaus is very forgiving we still have some standards.

  • Up and Coming Artist Preferred But All Artists Welcome
  • Competent Artist
  • Cares More For The Art And The Message Than Money
  • Art That Has A Message
  • Of Course The Art Has To Be Beautiful

How It Works?

  1. You Make An ArtPiece (photography & paintings only)
  2. Email Us A Request (with an artist description) That You Would Like To Sell Your Artpiece With A High Quality Picture
  3. We Email You A Terms & Conditons Paper To Sign
  4. You Send Back Your Digital Signature
  5. We List Your Item With A Price You Determine
  6. Customer Buys Your Artpiece
  7. We Email You The Shipping Label And You Ship It Yourself
  8. We Send You Your Share Of The Payment (Royalties 5%)
